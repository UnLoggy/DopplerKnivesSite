Doppler knivies, CS.Money Blog article

Counter-Strike 2 has a wide variety of knife skins with almost two dozen options available. And Doppler is one of the most popular and beautiful knife skins, which only got better after the game's transition from CS:GO to CS2.

Doppler Knives have four numbered Phases and three separate Styles. Doppler Phases are simply numbered one through four, while Styles are Ruby, Sapphire and Black Pearl.

What is a Doppler Phase? It's a name to a group of certain Doppler knife skins that look similar and in the same colour pallette. Each Phase combines a set of colour on Doppler Knives, depending on the mix of red and blue and how they look together.

Each Doppler Phase has its own price category. Most often, their price tag changes from lower to higher in this order: Phase #1, Phase #3, Phase #4 and Phase #2. So, to answer which Doppler Phase is most expensive, it's Phase 1. Also, there may be exceptions to this order, depending on rare patterns.
